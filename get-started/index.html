    What to Prepare Before Your First Agency Call

    • Know your budget range. Total costs typically run $80,000–$180,000.
    • Clarify your embryo situation. Do you have frozen embryos? Will you need IVF or donor eggs/sperm?
    • Think about surrogate preferences. Contact level, geographic preferences, delivery state?
    • Consult a reproductive attorney first. Before signing any agency agreement, speak with a licensed reproductive attorney in your target delivery state.
