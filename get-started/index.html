What to Prepare Before Your First Agency Call

1
Know your budget range. Total costs typically run $80,000–$180,000. Have a realistic number in mind before your first agency conversation.
2
Clarify your embryo situation. Do you have frozen embryos ready? Will you need IVF? Will you need donor eggs, donor sperm, or both?
3
Think about surrogate preferences. What level of contact do you want during the pregnancy? Geographic preferences? Delivery state?
4
Consult a reproductive attorney first. Before signing any agency agreement, speak with a licensed reproductive attorney in your target delivery state. They can review agency contracts and set realistic legal expectations.

Professional Resources for Intended Parents

RESOLVE: The National Infertility Association

National nonprofit providing education, advocacy, and community support for all paths to parenthood including surrogacy. Provider directory at resolve.org.

Men Having Babies

Nonprofit focused specifically on gay men pursuing surrogacy. Education events, advisor network, and financial assistance grants at menhavingbabies.org.

ASRM — American Society for Reproductive Medicine

Professional organization for fertility specialists. Guidelines on surrogacy and third-party reproduction at asrm.org.

Academy of Adoption & Assisted Reproduction Attorneys

Find reproductive attorneys experienced in surrogacy in your delivery state at adoptionart.org.
