What to Prepare Before Your First Agency Call

1
Know your budget range. Total costs typically run $80K–$180K. Know roughly what you can commit to before your first agency conversation.
2
Know your embryo situation. Do you already have frozen embryos? Will you need IVF? Will you need donor eggs or sperm? Agencies will ask.
3
Think about your surrogate preferences. What level of contact do you want during the pregnancy? Geographic preference? These will shape your matching experience.
4
Have questions ready. Ask about surrogate screening process, average matching time, re-matching policies, and whether they've worked with families like yours before.
