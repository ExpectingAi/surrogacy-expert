    About This Site

    About Surrogacy.expert

    A free, independent educational resource for intended parents pursuing gestational surrogacy in the United States — no ads, no commercial interests.

    Our Mission

    The path to parenthood through surrogacy is one of the most meaningful — and most overwhelming — journeys a person can undertake. Intended parents deserve clear, honest, independent information that helps them make confident decisions at every stage.

    Surrogacy.expert was built to fill a gap: most surrogacy information online comes from agencies or fertility clinics with a commercial interest in your decision. We have no such interest. Our only goal is to help you understand the process clearly and make the right decisions for your family.

    What This Site Covers

    • What gestational surrogacy is and how it works
    • The complete step-by-step process from agency selection to birth
    • Honest cost breakdowns for every line item
    • Legal frameworks in all 50 states and DC
    • Guides for LGBTQ+ families, single parents, and international intended parents
    • How to find, evaluate, and work with a surrogate
    • The IVF and embryo transfer process
    • How to talk to your child about their surrogacy story

    Editorial Standards

    All content on Surrogacy.expert is written to be accurate, current, and independent. We do not accept advertising. We do not receive compensation from agencies, fertility clinics, or attorneys in exchange for recommendations or mentions.

    Important: Surrogacy.expert provides educational information only. Nothing on this site constitutes legal, medical, or financial advice. Always consult a licensed reproductive attorney, qualified medical professional, and financial advisor before making decisions about your surrogacy journey.
