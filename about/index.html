About This Site

About Surrogacy.expert

Surrogacy.expert is a free, independent educational resource for intended parents pursuing gestational surrogacy in the United States.

Our Mission

The path to parenthood through surrogacy is one of the most meaningful — and most overwhelming — journeys a person can undertake. Intended parents deserve clear, honest, independent information that helps them make confident decisions at every stage.

Surrogacy.expert was built to fill a gap: most surrogacy information online comes from agencies or fertility clinics with a commercial interest in your decision. We have no such interest. Our only goal is to help you understand the process clearly and make the right decisions for your family.

What This Site Covers

We cover every aspect of gestational surrogacy for intended parents:

  • What gestational surrogacy is and how it works
  • The complete step-by-step process from agency selection to birth
  • Honest cost breakdowns for every line item
  • Legal frameworks in all 50 states and DC
  • Guides for LGBTQ+ families, single parents, and international intended parents
  • How to find, evaluate, and work with a surrogate
  • The IVF and embryo transfer process
  • How to talk to your child about their surrogacy story

Editorial Standards

All content on Surrogacy.expert is written to be accurate, current, and independent. We do not accept advertising. We do not receive compensation from agencies, fertility clinics, or attorneys in exchange for recommendations or mentions. We do not have affiliate relationships that affect our editorial content.

Surrogacy law and practice evolve. We update our content regularly to reflect current statutes, case law developments, and best practices. If you find something that appears outdated or inaccurate, please let us know.

⚠️ Important: Surrogacy.expert provides educational information only. Nothing on this site constitutes legal, medical, or financial advice. Surrogacy law varies significantly by state and country. Always consult a licensed reproductive attorney, qualified medical professional, and financial advisor before making decisions about your surrogacy journey.

Key Professional Organizations

For professional guidance, we recommend:

  • RESOLVE: The National Infertility Association — resolve.org — Education, advocacy, and professional directory
  • Academy of Adoption & Assisted Reproduction Attorneys (AAAA) — adoptionart.org — Find experienced reproductive attorneys
  • American Society for Reproductive Medicine (ASRM) — asrm.org — Clinical guidelines and physician directory
  • Men Having Babies — menhavingbabies.org — Focused resources for gay men pursuing surrogacy
  • Family Equality — familyequality.org — Advocacy and community for LGBTQ+ families
