About This Site

About IntendedParents.Expert

Surrogacy.expert is a free educational resource created by Expecting.AI — an AI-powered fertility platform connecting intended parents with vetted surrogacy professionals across the United States.

Our Mission

The path to parenthood through surrogacy is one of the most meaningful journeys a person can undertake — and one of the most overwhelming to navigate alone. Intended parents deserve clear, honest, comprehensive information that helps them make confident decisions at every stage of their journey.

Surrogacy.expert was built to be the resource we wish existed: comprehensive enough to answer every major question, honest about the complexity and cost of the process, and organized around the way real intended parents actually think about their journey.

Our Parent Organization: Expecting.AI

Expecting.AI is an AI-powered fertility platform covering surrogacy, egg donation, sperm donation, and fertility provider discovery. With 200+ providers in network, 100+ babies born through partner journeys, and a team dedicated to making family building more accessible and transparent, Expecting.AI is the operational partner behind this educational resource.

Editorial Standards

All content on Surrogacy.expert is reviewed for accuracy and updated regularly. We do not accept advertising, and we do not receive compensation from agencies, clinics, or attorneys in exchange for recommendations or mentions. Our only goal is to help intended parents navigate their journey with confidence.

⚠️ Surrogacy.expert provides educational information only. Nothing on this site constitutes legal, medical, or financial advice. Always consult qualified professionals before making decisions about your surrogacy journey.
