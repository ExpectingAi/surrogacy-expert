How to Evaluate a State for Surrogacy

When choosing a state for your surrogacy arrangement, the key legal factors are: whether the state has a formal statute or relies on case law; whether pre-birth orders (PBOs) are routinely issued; whether PBOs are available for all family types (including same-sex couples and single parents); and the reliability and consistency of legal outcomes in that state.
